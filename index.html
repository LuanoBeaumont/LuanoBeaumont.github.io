About Me

I am a student of Applied Mathematics, but my journey into complex systems began with a creative spark: building 3D assets and even attempting to construct a complete horror game. This background in game development gave me an appreciation for how intricate rules and systems work together to create a cohesive experience. Today, I channel that same curiosity into my academic pursuits in Operations Research, exploring how non-linear and integer programming can optimize outcomes in the real world. I bring these abstract models to life using software like AMPL and am always eager to learn new tools like GAMS.

This blend of creative and analytical thinking defines my skillset. I am proficient in a range of programming languages, from the low-level control of C++ and the statistical power of R to the versatile scripting of Python. I believe this diverse technical foundation, which also includes creating professional documentation in LaTeX, is uniquely suited for a career in finance. Outside of my academic and technical work, I find balance in creative outlets like cooking Italian food for my loved ones, playing soccer for fun, and enjoying quiet hikes along the creek with my dog Tomo back home. I love my cat 💖🐈
